Hi! My name is Semaj.

I was born in Philadelphia, PA. I was a premature baby, born in a “coma vigil” state. I had medical obstacles including seizures, moderate hearing loss, and speech impairment. As a toddler I went to physical and speech therapy, at least four times a week.

Today, I can move freely and speak, and I am no longer required to take medication for seizure episodes. I may have to wear hearing aids, but it does not stop me from being who I am destined to be. I am a Distinguished Honor student, I love to play sports (mainly basketball and football), I enjoy listening to music, and spending time with my family and friends. I hope to inspire others with special needs to embrace their lives as well.

Thanks for visiting and don't forget to check out my store!
